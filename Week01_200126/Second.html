BJP Governments at the Centre (India)

No. Period / Term Government Formed by BJP / BJP-led NDA Type of Government
1 1996 (May – June) BJP under Atal Bihari Vajpayee – lasted ~13 days Short coalition (no majority)

4 2014 – 2019 BJP-led NDA (Narendra Modi) Majority government
5 2019 – 2024 BJP-led NDA (Narendra Modi) Majority government
