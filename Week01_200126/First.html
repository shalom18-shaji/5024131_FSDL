The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) is one of India’s two major national political parties and currently the dominant political force at the Centre. It emerged as a strong national party in the 1990s and reshaped Indian politics by moving away from coalition dependency.

1980: BJP founded.
1996: First government under Atal Bihari Vajpayee.
2014: Historic majority victory.
2019 & 2024: Continued dominance through NDA.
