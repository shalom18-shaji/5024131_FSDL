Narendra Modi – Elections as Prime Minister

  • 2014: Elected as Prime Minister for the first time (Term: 2014–2019, Full majority – BJP majority on its own)
  • 2019: Re-elected as Prime Minister for the second time (Term: 2019–2024, Full majority – BJP majority on its own)
  • 2024: Elected as Prime Minister for the third time (Term: 2024–Present, BJP-led NDA government)
